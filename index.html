FAQ

Good questions

Do you see my Telegram or broker credentials?

No. CopyPilot runs on your machine; your Telegram session and MT5 login never leave your PC.

Which signal channels are supported?

Any channel we've built a parser for. Each provider's format is different, and we keep parsers updated as they change.

Does it run 24/7?

It runs whenever your PC (or a VPS) is on. For round-the-clock copying we recommend a low-cost Forex VPS.

Is this financial advice?

No. CopyPilot is an execution tool. Trading carries substantial risk of loss — you are responsible for your own decisions.
